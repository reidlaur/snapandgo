Introducing Snap and Go, the simple and easy solution to never losing your transport card again.

Using the technology of Near Field Communication, Snap and Go aims to take away the hassle of losing, replacing and digging through an assortment of cards.

Pairing with most wearable technology devices, an easy wave of the wrist gets you on-board and on track to your destination.

snap. and go.

Target Audience

Snap and Go is aimed at current wearable technology users but also has focus on the future elderly population as it is a simple way to make public transportation use hassle-free. The assurance that your money is physically with you instead of sitting on a card that is easy to drop or get lost creates a feeling of reassurance and security within the technology.
